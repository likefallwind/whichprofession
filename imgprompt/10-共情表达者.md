# 10 · 共情表达者 (Empathic Voice) · A-S

## 角色定位
通过表达、审美、故事和教育影响他人。
关键词：共情 / 表达 / 感染力。

## 画面创意
一个温暖、富有感染力的年轻表达者，面带真诚笑容，一手做出讲述/分享的手势，身边有一张小海报、
一个对话气泡和好几颗爱心。整体传达「用故事和温度去打动人」。

## 主提示词（English · 直接交给 AI）
> Flat hand-drawn doodle-style illustration of a warm expressive young storyteller
> character with a sincere smile, one hand making an open sharing gesture. A small poster,
> a speech bubble and several hearts float nearby. Heartfelt engaging expression, simple
> rounded shapes, thick slightly wobbly hand-drawn ink outlines, crayon and marker texture,
> minimal flat shading. Warm cream paper background with subtle grain. Limited palette
> dominated by leaf green (#5A8030) accents over a pale sage (#D9E6C9) tone. Scattered
> small doodle stars, sparkles, squiggles and dots. Playful, optimistic, sticker-like,
> centered bust composition, clean negative space, vector-friendly, no photorealism.
