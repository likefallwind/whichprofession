# 02 · 造物设计师 (Builder Designer) · R-A

## 角色定位
既要做出来，也要做得好看、好用、有体验感。
关键词：造物感 / 美学 / 体验。

## 画面创意
一个气质温和的年轻设计师，一手拿铅笔在草图本上画产品/建筑草图，另一手托着一个小巧的实体模型
（比如一把椅子或一栋小房子模型）。画面同时出现「草图线条」和「立体成品」，体现从设计到造物的过程。
点缀直尺、色卡、波浪线。

## 主提示词（English · 直接交给 AI）
> Flat hand-drawn doodle-style illustration of a gentle young designer character, one hand
> sketching a product on a drawing pad, the other hand holding a small physical model of a
> chair or tiny house. Doodle sketch lines transform into a solid finished object. A ruler,
> color swatches and curved squiggles float nearby. Calm creative expression, simple
> rounded shapes, thick slightly wobbly hand-drawn ink outlines, crayon and marker texture,
> minimal flat shading. Warm cream paper background with subtle grain. Limited palette
> dominated by terracotta clay-rose (#B05A4F) accents over a warm sand (#F4DBC4) tone.
> Scattered small doodle stars, sparkles, squiggles and dots. Playful, optimistic,
> sticker-like, centered bust composition, clean negative space, vector-friendly, no
> photorealism.

- 背景柔色：暖砂 #F4DBC4
