# 09 · 数据侦探 (Data Detective) · I-C

## 角色定位
喜欢从数据、证据、规则和模型里找答案。
关键词：数据感 / 逻辑感 / 证据感。

## 画面创意
一个机敏、专注的年轻数据侦探，戴着侦探帽，手举一个大放大镜，镜中放大显示出柱状图/数据点。
身边有线索连线、问号、对勾，传达「从数据里找到真相」。

## 主提示词（English · 直接交给 AI）
> Flat hand-drawn doodle-style illustration of an alert focused young data detective
> character wearing a detective hat, holding up a large magnifying glass that reveals a
> magnified bar chart and data dots inside the lens. Doodle clue lines, question marks and
> checkmarks float nearby. Clever inquisitive expression, simple rounded shapes, thick
> slightly wobbly hand-drawn ink outlines, crayon and marker texture, minimal flat shading.
> Warm cream paper background with subtle grain. Limited palette dominated by deep navy
> (#1E4480) accents over a light steel-blue (#D6E2F3) tone. Scattered small doodle stars,
> sparkles, squiggles and dots. Playful, optimistic, sticker-like, centered bust
> composition, clean negative space, vector-friendly, no photorealism.
