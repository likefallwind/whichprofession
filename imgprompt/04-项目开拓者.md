# 04 · 项目开拓者 (Project Pioneer) · R-E

## 角色定位
喜欢行动、资源整合、项目推进和结果负责。
关键词：推进 / 整合 / 落地。

## 画面创意
一个充满干劲、向前迈步的年轻开拓者，一手高举一面手绘小旗子（象征里程碑），
另一手拿着项目计划板。身边有打包好的箱子、一架小无人机、向上的箭头，体现「把项目推到落地」。

## 主提示词（English · 直接交给 AI）
> Flat hand-drawn doodle-style illustration of an energetic young pioneer character
> striding forward, one hand raising a doodle flag like a milestone marker, the other hand
> holding a project planning board. Stacked delivery boxes, a tiny drone and upward arrows
> float nearby. Confident driven expression, simple rounded shapes, thick slightly wobbly
> hand-drawn ink outlines, crayon and marker texture, minimal flat shading. Warm cream
> paper background with subtle grain. Limited palette dominated by amber honey (#B07820)
> accents over a light cream-gold (#FAE8C5) tone. Scattered small doodle stars, sparkles,
> squiggles and dots. Playful, optimistic, sticker-like, centered bust composition, clean
> negative space, vector-friendly, no photorealism.
